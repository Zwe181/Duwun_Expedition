What is Duwun?

Duwun means North Star in Burmese. The every true north, served as the primary navigation assist for millennials. At Duwun, we redefine exploration by blending the untamed beauty of nature with the elegance of luxury. Specializing in curated journeys across Canada's breathtaking landscapes, Duwun offers exclusive experiences like witnessing the ethereal Northern Lights, embarking on Arctic cruises, and intimate city and park tours. Every adventure with Duwun promises not just an escape, but a refined immersion into the wilderness, ensuring that every moment is both exhilarating and indulgent.
