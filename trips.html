Itinerary

11-Day Eastern Canada Extravaganza

Join us on an extraordinary journey through Eastern Canada, exploring the enchanting cities of Québec City, Montréal, Ottawa, and beyond! This 11-day adventure is perfect for history buffs, festival-goers, and photography enthusiasts alike.

Day 1-3: Québec City

Begin your journey in the charming Québec City. Wander through Old Québec, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and snap stunning photos of Château Frontenac. Dive into the local culture at the Québec Winter Carnival or the Summer Festival. Discover the rich history and enjoy delightful cuisine.

Day 4-5: Charlevoix

Travel to the picturesque Charlevoix region. Enjoy scenic drives along the St. Lawrence River, visit charming villages, and explore the beautiful landscapes. Engage in outdoor activities like hiking and whale watching.

Day 6-7: Montréal

Next, explore the vibrant city of Montréal. Attend world-class festivals like the Montréal Jazz Festival or Just for Laughs. Wander through the eclectic neighborhoods of Plateau Mont-Royal and Old Montréal, and marvel at the stunning Notre-Dame Basilica. Relish the city’s culinary delights and dynamic nightlife.

Day 8: Mont-Tremblant

Head to Mont-Tremblant, known for its stunning natural beauty. Whether it's hiking, skiing, or simply enjoying the scenic views, Mont-Tremblant offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation.

Day 9-10: Ottawa

Continue to Canada's capital, Ottawa. Witness the Changing of the Guard on Parliament Hill, explore the Canadian Museum of History, and take in the scenic beauty along the Rideau Canal. Experience the vibrant Tulip Festival in spring or the ice sculptures and skating during Winterlude.

DSay 11: Kingston and the Thousand Islands

Conclude your trip with a visit to Kingston and the Thousand Islands. Explore the historic sites of Kingston and enjoy a picturesque boat tour through the Thousand Islands, capturing the breathtaking scenery.

This comprehensive itinerary ensures you experience the best of Eastern Canada, from historic sites and cultural festivals to stunning natural landscapes. Book now and create unforgettable memories with us!
